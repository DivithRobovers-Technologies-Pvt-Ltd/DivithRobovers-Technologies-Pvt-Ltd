The DivithRoboverse Ecosystem: Learn, Partner, and Grow

We offer three high-impact models designed for maximum reach and results, catering to students, institutions, and business partners.

Roboverse School Integration (B2B)

Focus & Clarity

Complete Curriculum Outsourcing: We partner with schools to embed Robotics, AI, Coding, and Drones into the daily syllabus (Grades 1-9). We supply certified instructors, proprietary materials, and a structured, evolving curriculum.

Core Value Proposition

Future-Proof Your School: We make advanced technology education accessible and mandatory, ensuring every student acquires essential digital-age skills.

Roboverse Learning Hubs (B2C)

Focus & Clarity

Specialized Skill Mastery: Our centers offer 23 focused courses across 8 technologies: Robotics, AI (Python), IoT, 3D Printing, Drones, App Development, Scratch Coding, and Aviation. Ideal for deep, one-on-one mastery outside of school hours.

Core Value Proposition

Unleash Core Abilities: Designed to significantly boost a student's Observation Skills, Ability to Think, and Creativity Skills.

Robo Kits & At-Home Learning

Focus & Clarity

Innovation on Demand: The 'My Robo Box' kits are proprietary, hands-on learning systems. They are structured for self-paced learning and project-based experimentation at home.

Core Value Proposition

Engage, Don't Distract: A positive, constructive alternative to passive screen time, transforming your child's home into a personal innovation lab.
